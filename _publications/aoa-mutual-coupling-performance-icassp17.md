This paper accompanies the proposed AoA estimator for uniform linear arrays with extended mutual-coupling parameters with a detailed theoretical performance analysis. Closed-form expressions for the estimator's bias and variance are derived and benchmarked against the corresponding Cramer-Rao bound.
