This work introduces DRIP, a versatile family of space-time discrete-time sequences designed for integrated sensing and communication (ISAC) systems. The construction provides flexible trade-offs between communication rate, sensing accuracy, and practical hardware constraints, making it broadly applicable across ISAC waveform-design scenarios.
