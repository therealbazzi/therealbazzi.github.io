This paper develops a hypothesis-testing framework that compares FMCW and OFDM waveforms for joint communication and radar (JCR) in IEEE 802.11bd vehicular networks. The analysis quantifies the trade-offs between the two waveforms for combined target detection and data transmission in the 802.11bd setting.
