This work formulates a minimum-power transmit-design problem for secure full-duplex integrated sensing and communication. The proposed design simultaneously satisfies a secrecy constraint with respect to a malicious eavesdropper-target and a sensing accuracy constraint, while minimizing the transmit power budget.
