This paper designs integrated sensing and communication (ISAC) waveforms in which the peak-to-average power ratio (PAPR) is treated as a tunable design knob. The resulting family of waveforms allows operators to trade off PAPR against sensing accuracy and communication rate, accommodating the linearity constraints of practical RF hardware.
