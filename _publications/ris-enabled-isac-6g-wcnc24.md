This work proposes a reconfigurable intelligent surface (RIS) enabled integrated sensing and communication (ISAC) scheme for 6G systems. The RIS configuration is jointly designed to support both sensing and communication objectives, demonstrating the role of programmable propagation environments in next-generation integrated systems.
