This invited paper develops a robust beamforming method for dual-functional radar-communication (DFRC) systems operating under channel-state-information uncertainty. The accompanying analysis offers design insights into the trade-offs between sensing accuracy, communication rate, and robustness, motivating choices made in subsequent ISAC beamforming designs.
