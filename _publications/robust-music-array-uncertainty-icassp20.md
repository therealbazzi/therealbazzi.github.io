This work develops a robust MUSIC angle-of-arrival estimator that maintains performance under array response uncertainty — a setting where the classical MUSIC estimator can degrade significantly. The proposed approach accounts for unknown mutual coupling and steering-vector perturbations, yielding consistent estimates in realistic antenna-array deployments.
