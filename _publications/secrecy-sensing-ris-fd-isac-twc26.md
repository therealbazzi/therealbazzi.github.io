This work studies a reconfigurable intelligent surface (RIS) assisted, full-duplex integrated sensing and communication network in the presence of eavesdroppers. A joint secrecy–sensing optimization framework is developed, trading off communication confidentiality against sensing accuracy under realistic transmit-power and RIS-configuration constraints.
