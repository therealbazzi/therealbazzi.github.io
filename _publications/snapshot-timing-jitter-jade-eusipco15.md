This paper analyzes how random snapshot timing jitter perturbs the sample covariance matrix used in joint angle and delay estimation (JADE). Closed-form expressions are derived for the perturbation, and the resulting accuracy loss in downstream JADE estimators is quantified, informing acquisition-system design choices.
