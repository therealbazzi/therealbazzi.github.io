This paper develops an iterative variational Bayes algorithm for sparse signal recovery and applies it to angle-of-arrival (AoA) estimation. The Bayesian formulation produces uncertainty quantification alongside point estimates, which is useful in operational settings where confidence bounds matter as much as the AoA values themselves.
