This work introduces a waveform-design framework for ISAC systems in which the peak-to-average power ratio (PAPR) is treated as a tunable parameter. The design preserves sensing and communication performance across a range of PAPR targets, easing the impact of high-PAPR modern waveforms on practical RF hardware.
